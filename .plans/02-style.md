# Plan: Style Refinement

## Objective
Refine the EverGray Tech website so it presents a more elegant, sophisticated, and company-centered brand experience while improving readability, accessibility, and overall polish.

## Customer Value
- Strengthens trust and professionalism through a more refined visual presentation
- Improves readability and usability for visitors across devices and contexts
- Better positions EverGray Tech as the primary brand rather than centering the individual founder

## Scope Decisions (Locked)
- Favor restraint, clarity, and sophistication over flashy visual effects
- Keep EverGray Tech as the primary brand, with leadership as supporting context
- Improve accessibility and readability wherever styling changes are made
- Preserve a premium feel without adding unnecessary complexity
- Leave larger future expansions, such as leadership pages and advanced presentation features, for later phases

## Prerequisites
- `01-mvp.md`
